# Rathmines College: Data Security Policy Framework

## 🛡️ Executive Summary
This policy outlines the technical and administrative safeguards required to protect the personal data of 750+ students and staff. It ensures full compliance with **GDPR** and the **Irish Data Protection Act 2018**, focusing on the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of college information resources.

## 📖 Key Policy Components

### 1. Regulatory Compliance
*   **GDPR (EU 2016/679):** Standardizing data privacy across the EU.
*   **Data Protection Act 2018:** Implementing national provisions in Irish law.
*   **ePrivacy Directive:** Governing electronic communications and cookies.

### 2. Risk Management & Cybersecurity
The policy identifies and addresses critical vulnerabilities, including:
*   Phishing and Malware mitigation.
*   Credential security (Password protocols).
*   Backup integrity (Transitioning from weekly to high-frequency cycles).

### 3. Data Lifecycle Management
Detailed protocols for the entire data journey:
*   **Storage:** AES encryption for sensitive health and financial records.
*   **Access:** Implementation of Subject Access Request (SAR) procedures with 30-day response windows.
*   **Disposal:** Secure shredding and permanent digital deletion methods.

## 👥 Stakeholder Responsibilities
*   **IT Management:** Firewalls, access controls, and threat monitoring.
*   **Users (Staff/Students):** Account accountability and incident reporting.
*   **Incident Response Team:** Breach investigation and Data Protection Commission (DPC) notification.
